Who are your temporal relatives?

Find out who they are.




A coexisting existence


It's really easy to find out who your temporal relatives are. They are everywhere. They're all of the 7 billion people of this earth, but on one condition: they have to have been alive at the time that you were alive. A temporal relative is anyone whom you can or ever could interact with, or in other words, coexist with in the same universe. For instance, if you were born in the 21st century, it is very unlikely that Albert Einstein is and will ever be your temporal relative, since you both never coexisted at the same time. But an old grandpa born in the 1940's can call Einstein his temporal relative, since both beings once coexisted in the same world.


Less than 10% of all of the humans will ever be your temporal relatives. That's just to show how many of us ever lived, which is estimated to be around 100 billion. The percentage of people that are temporal relatives to each other has been increasing exponentially, considering the populational boom in the last century.
